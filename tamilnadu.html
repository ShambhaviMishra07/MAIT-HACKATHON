Tamil Nadu

Heritage Sites

Meenakshi Temple

Located in Madurai, this temple is dedicated to Goddess Meenakshi (Parvati) and Lord Sundareswarar (Shiva). Known for its majestic gopurams (towers) decorated with thousands of colorful sculptures. The Meenakshi Tirukalyanam festival is a grand celebration held annually.

Rameswaram Temple

A sacred Hindu temple located on Rameswaram Island. One of the Char Dham pilgrimage sites. Famous for its long corridors, intricately carved pillars, and sacred wells.

Festivals

Pongal

The harvest festival of Tamil Nadu, celebrated in January. It is a four-day festival: Bhogi, Thai Pongal, Mattu Pongal, and Kaanum Pongal. People prepare Pongal (sweet rice dish) and offer it to the Sun God.

Thaipusam

A Hindu festival celebrated in Tamil Nadu and by Tamil communities worldwide. Devotees carry Kavadi (burdens) as a symbol of their devotion to Lord Murugan. Many perform extreme rituals like piercing their bodies with hooks and skewers.
