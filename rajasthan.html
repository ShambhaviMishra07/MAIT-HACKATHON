Heritage Sites

Amer Fort

A majestic fort in Jaipur, built by Raja Man Singh in 1592. It features Rajput and Mughal architecture with intricate carvings. The fort has attractions like Sheesh Mahal (Mirror Palace) and Diwan-e-Khas. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Festivals

Pushkar Camel Fair

An annual fair in Pushkar, Rajasthan, where thousands of camels, horses, and cattle are traded. The fair also includes cultural performances, camel races, and traditional competitions. It is one of the most famous livestock fairs in the world.

Gangaur Festival

A festival dedicated to Goddess Gauri (Parvati), celebrated mainly by women. Women pray for the well-being of their husbands and a happy married life. Celebrations include colorful processions, folk dances, and traditional rituals.
